About The App

The purpose of Crickets is to provide private internal communication for companies and organizations. The app features the ablility to share updates for all registered users on the homepage thread. In addition to being able to communicate , employees are also able to reserve conference rooms and see what times are available. HR will also be able to leave employee reviews in our reviews site.
